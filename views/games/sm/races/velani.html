Velani

Also called Kasathas, grey-skinned humanoids with four arms and pure black eyes. Known for a great veneration of history and "great families", their written records extend further back than any other. This leads to a culture steeped in customs that can seem bizarre to outsiders, such as the keeping hidden of one’s mouth around all but one’s most intimate companions.

Origin: Vland

Specialties: Tactics, Engineering, and Melee
