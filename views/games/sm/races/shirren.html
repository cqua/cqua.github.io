Shirren

Shirrens were once a monstrous swarm. Centuries ago, however, a mysterious mutation caused an entire subcolony to break from the hivemind, with each of its members gaining a sense of self. In addition to their two sets of main limbs, they also have two sets of smaller limbs growing from their thoraxes. While often displayed, these “mating arms” are extremely weak and used only for ceremonial and reproductive purposes—to use them for mundane activities would be seen as grotesque and shameful.

Origin: Kyre

Specialties: Melee, Navigation, and Survival
