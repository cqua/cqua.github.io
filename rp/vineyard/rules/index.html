Skills

Each character has five skills, a number between 0 and 5, which represents how adept they are at that skill:

Grit (GR) is a character's physical prowess. It measures strength, endurance, and resistance to physical ailments. Use Grit when wielding a heavy tool, running a long distance, intimidating an enemy, fighting off a poison.

Finesse (FN) is a character's physical fidelity. It measures flexibility, reaction speed, and ability to dodge. Use Finesse when using a fine tool, moving stealthily, telling a convincing lie, or avoiding a physical danger.

Acuity (AC) is a character's mental clarity. It measures logical reasoning, and ability to read emotions and intentions. Use Acuity when researching, scanning the horizon, or or working with a machine.

Sense (SN) is a character's perception. It measures ability to percieve and quickly interpret the sights, sounds, and finer details of their environment. Use Sense when scanning the horizon, listening for footsteps, or reading body language.
