Daytime Actions

Every Dawn:

  • Each PC who was not downed the previous night reduces all harm and takes 1xp.
  • Each PC suffers Exhausted (Level-1 Harm).
  • Each PC who was downed must successfully Rest before they can take any other actions or participate in activities.
During the day, each PC is able to take two actions- one during the morning, and one during the afternoon. It's in everyone's best interest to have returned by the evening. If multiple PCs perform the same action at the same time of day, they can choose to form a team. If a PC chooses an area further than 5 miles away from base as part of a Daytime action, they must spend their second action travelling to and from the area. PC's cannot choose areas further than 10 miles away.

Scavenge.

Choose an area. Roll on your best skill, then:

  • On a 4/5, choose one:
    • You find supply, but you take a level-2 harm of the Referee's choice.
    • You find supply with -1 effect.
    • You lose the equipment you used for the roll.
  • On a 6, you find supply.
  • On a Critical Success, choose one:
    • You gain supply, and a useful item of the Referee's choice.
    • You gain supply with +1 effect.
    • You gain supply and find a place to rest. Reduce one harm.
When you find supply:
  • 0 Effect Nothing.
  • 1 Effect 1 Supply.
  • 2 Effect 1d3 Supply.
  • 3 Effect 2d3 Supply.
  • 4+ Effect 2d3 Supply and you find an item of your choice.

Survey.

Choose an area made up of up to 5 zones. Then roll Sense, taking -1d for each zone in the area beyond the first:

  • On a 4/5, choose one:
    • You map out the area, but you take a level-2 harm of the Referee's choice. Ask the Referee one question.
    • You map out the area, but you lose the equipment you used for the roll. Ask the Referee one question.
  • On a 6, map out the area.
  • On a Critical Success, choose one:
    • You map out the area, and a useful item of the Referee's choice.
    • You map out the area and find 1d3 Supply.
    • You map out the area and find a place to rest. Reduce one harm.

Downed PCs

A PC who has been downed must successfully rest before they can act during the day or night. Depending on where a player was downed, a Scavenge or Raid action may be required to return the downed PC to base.

Working as a Team

When two or more PCs take an action together, they can choose to act as a Team by choosing a Leader.

Whenever the action calls for a roll, each member of the team attempts the roll. The result is the highest roll, but the Leader suffers Level-1 harm of the Referee's choice for each PC who rolled 1-3 as their best result.
