Daytime Actions

Every Dawn:

  • Each player who was not downed the previous night reduces all harm and takes 1xp.
  • Each player suffers Hungry (Level-1 Harm).
During the day, each player is able to take two actions- one during the morning and one during the afternoon. If multiple players perform the same action at the same time of day, they can choose to form a team.

Scavenge for Supplies.

Roll on your best skill, then:

  • On a 4/5, choose one:
    • You find supply, but you take a level-2 harm of the Referee's choice.
    • You find supply with -1 effect.
    • You return empty-handed, having lost any equipment you used for the roll.
  • On a 6, you find supply.
  • On a Critical Success, choose one:
    • You gain supply, and a useful item of the Referee's choice.
    • You gain supply with +1 effect.
    • You gain supply and find a place to rest. Reduce one harm.
When you find supply:
  • 0 Effect Nothing.
  • 1 Effect 1 Supply.
  • 2 Effect 1d3 Supply.
  • 3 Effect 2d3 Supply.
  • 4+ Effect 2d3 Supply and you find an item of your choice.

Working as a Team

When two or more characters take an action together, they can choose to act as a Team by choosing a Leader.

Whenever the action calls for a roll, each member of the team attempts the roll. The result is the highest roll, but the Leader suffers Level-1 harm of the Referee's choice for each character who rolled 1-3 as their best result.
