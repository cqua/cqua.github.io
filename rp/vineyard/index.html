Campaign Overview

The forest moon quietly orbits the gas giant Chiros...

Surveyed 400 years ago and unsuccessfully colonized 200 years ago, the moon's slow, creeping flora has claimed the remnants of human civilization. For unspecified reasons, the planet was restricted from human use and designated Uninhabitable.

This was unremarkable. Many environments had this designation.

But when a ship of 200 people crashes on the surface of this moon, it's passengers will have to succeed where the colony failed, or suffer their fate.

The Twelve Stars

The Twelve Stars is the coalition of fourteen star systems (because nothing is simple) which contain the majority of human life. After the Great Diaspora where humans left the Earth en masse, five major colonies had been established. This time is called the First Era.
