Broker

One of the few jobs that can never be overtaken by AI. You're a people person, a leader. On a Core world, you might be an Administrator or a cultural head, ensuring everyone under your responsibility has their every need met. The resources are always there- that's post-scarcity- but it's up to you to manage them efficiently.

Out on the Rim, however, you might be a business negotiator, a religious leader, or a politician. Out in the Rim, you're less likely to have responsibilities and more likely to have opportunities, for wealth or popularity.

Courier

The further away people get from one-another, the more people are needed to bridge the gap. Perhaps you live on a cruiser, hopping from ring to ring, delivering what needs delivering. Modern ships can sustain entire lifetimes, and many would rather accept that than risk losing a home they love to time-dilation- it's a well-known fact that a ship travelling near-lightspeed will age at a tenth of the rate a Core world does.

Alternatively, there are many runners needed out on the Rim. Whether you're vaulting between skyscrapers and airlifts on Shimana, or biking your way across Northstar, a courier's two objectives are speed, and reliability.
