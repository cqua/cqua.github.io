Priest

Othon Nikolaidis

Priests pledge service to a cause or supernatural being, and this informs their use of mana. Service gives priests purpose in the world.

Discovering their tradition is a spiritual experience for priests. They encounter a powerful presence during their travels, feel some sacred presence enter them while studying a text, or have a dream in which they are chosen to inherit great power. The initial experience sets these individuals on the priest’s path and gives them the power they need to further their cause.

Tradition stands at the center of priests’ identities. It shapes their behavior, gives them purpose, and reveals their way forward. Priests committed to the Gods use different kinds of magic from those who serve the late Emperor.

LEVEL 1 PRIEST

Attributes Increase two by 1
Characteristics Health +4, Power +1, Prepared Spells +1
Languages and Professions You either add a language you speak or learn to read a language you already speak. Also, add one profession or additional language.

Oath Tradition You learn one spellcasting tradition. This becomes your oath tradition.

Whenever you complete a rest, you prepare a number of spells equal to your Prepared Spells (minimum 1). They must be from one of your oath traditions, even if you later learn other traditions. When you prepare a spell, you gain castings of these spells as if you had learned them, and you lose those castings the next time you complete a rest. See Spellcasting for more information

Shared Recovery You can use an action to heal damage equal to your healing rate. Then, choose one creature other than you that is within short range. The target also heals damage equal to its healing rate. Once you use this talent, you cannot use it again until after you complete a rest.

LEVEL 2 PRIEST

Characteristics Health +4, Prepared Spells +2

Prayer When a creature within short range of you makes an attack roll or challenge roll, you can use a triggered action to grant 1 boon on the triggering roll.

LEVEL 5 EXPERT PRIEST

Characteristics Health +4, Power +1, Prepared Spells +1

Imbued Strike When you use Prayer to grant a creature 1 boon on an attack roll, the attack deals 1d6 extra damage.

LEVEL 8 MASTER PRIEST

Characteristics Health +4, Prepared Spells +1

Inspiring Prayer When you use Prayer on a creature other than yourself, you make attack rolls and challenge rolls with 1 boon for 1 round.

Improved Shared Recovery You can use Shared Recovery twice.
