Stormsoul

Rudy Siswanto

Stormsoul are mana given sentient form. When the land feels it is in danger, it can awaken its mana to create elementals. Usually these elementals return to slumber after a time, but for reasons nobody is really sure of, stormsouls tend to stick around long after.

The Calm After Stormsouls are solid materials such as clothes, metal, or stone given humanoid form by a swirling storm within them. Some gained physical form to accomplish a specific goal, while other just seek a peaceful life and connection with mortals. They tend to think very logically and have trouble understanding emotions. Heights and weights vary wildly, and they rarely retain material form longer than 40 years, though their consciousness is likely ancient.

Common Names: Names taken by stormsouls are often words from long-dead languages that only they remember. Exampled include Arenth, Kairex, Fonte, Gazeth, Jairaith, Navrok, Nox, Tel'nori, Xenghaus

CREATING A STORMSOUL

Starting Attributes Strength +1, Agility +1, Intellect +1, Will +1. Raise one attribute by 2 and two other attributes by 1.
Perception equals your Intellect
Defense equals 5 + our Agility
Health equals 8 + your Strength
Healing Rate equals one-quarter your Health, round down
Size 1/2 or 1, Speed 10, Power 0
Damage 0, Beasthood 0, Maximum Beasthood 5 + your Will
Language and Professions You speak the Common Tongue and the Language of Ancients. Pick two professions or languages.

Child of the Storm You take half damage rounded down from thunder and lightning.

Voltaic Spark As an action or a reaction on your turn to conjure the power of storms. Choose one:

  • Electricity surges through your arm. Your next unarmed attack or attack with a metal weapon deals an additional 1d6 damage.
  • Electricity surges through a metal object or body of water Size 4 or smaller you are touching. Each other creature the metal object, body of water, or you takes 1d6 damage.

LEVEL 4 EXPERT STORMSOUL

Characteristics Health +5

Enhanced Spark The damage from Voltaic Spark is increased to 2d6.
