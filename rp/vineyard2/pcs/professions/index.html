Starting Professions

  • Hunters are expert rangers trained in searching, tracking, and killing. Move stealthily and observe, a Hunter needs only one shot.
  • Reavers are built for Deathland survival. When their proficient scavenging isn't enough, they can survive on scraps. Ride the waste and cut down the opposition.
  • Spiders are tricksters, tinkerers, and masterminds. A crafty soul can find the utility of anything. Use words, tonics, arcane artifacts, a hidden blade, and if all else fails, explosives.
  • The Stranger has been shaped by the ghostfield. They don't fear it anymore, it answers to them. Seek out the strange forces and bend them to your will.
