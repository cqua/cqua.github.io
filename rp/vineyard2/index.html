Life in the Deathlands

Betwixt the few cities which dot Akoros, beyond the lightning barriers keeping Her Imperial citizens safe, stetches the Deathlands- that wasteland of petrified trees and choking miasma belonging to restless souls. The only reliable way of spanning that distance is via train, and even that has its failings.

So when a train does break down, and it's miniature lightning barrier fails, its passengers can only try their luck in the wastes.

The Game

THE LAST TRAIN TO DUSKWALL is a game about a group of daring survivors cutting through an abomination-ridden wasteland. There will be dangerous holdhouts, scavenging, jerry-rigging, weary strangers, deception, betrayal, victory, and death.

The object of the game is to find out: can these unlucky afflicted survive the teeming threats of roving abominations, nomadic hunting parties, heedless scavengers, starvation, and the dark void miasma.
