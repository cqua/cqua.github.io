The Orange Dusklights

Dawn

At the first hour of the day:

  • Each PC who was not downed the previous night reduces all harm and takes 1xp.
  • All PCs refresh their armor boxes.
  • Each PC suffers Fatigue (Level-1 Harm).

Then, players are free to discuss plans and prepare for the day.

Players who were downed the previous night may still be unconscious depending on the severity of their harm. They cannot perform any actions or tasks until they complete a Rest.

Daylight

During Daylight, PCs perform tasks around the area surrounding the Holdout. Each PC chooses two tasks to complete before evening.

The Map

The area surrounding the Holdout is divided into zones- representing areas of a unique biome, size, or interest. Each zone has several landmarks, either structures, unique sites, or potential Holdouts. The zone containing the PCs' Holdout is called the Holdout Zone.

When performing a task that requires a PC to choose a zone, they may only choose the Holdout Zone and zones adjacent to the Holdout Zone.

When performing a task that requires a PC to choose a landmark, they may only choose landmarks in the Holdout Zone and zones adjacent to the Holdout Zone.

If a task has a PC choose a location, they may choose a zone or a landmark.

Daylight Tasks

Raid • Craft • Scavenge • Survey • Travel • Rest

During the day, each PC is able to perform two tasks- one during the morning and one during the afternoon. If multiple PCs perform the same task together, they may form a team

The following are tasks one may take:

Raid

Declare the type: assault, stealth, or social. Raids follow Holdout phase mechanics.

Craft

Scavenge

Choose a location to scavenge, then roll on your best skill:

  • On a 4/5, choose one:
    • You find supply with -1 effect.
    • You find supply, but you take a level-2 harm of the Referee's choice.
    • You find supply, but lose any equipment used for the roll.
  • On a 6, you find supply.
  • On a Critical Success, choose one:
    • You find supply, and a useful item of the Referee's choice.
    • You find supply with +1 effect.
    • You find supply and find a place to rest. Reduce one harm.

When you find supply, determine the amount by effect level:

  • 0 Effect Nothing.
  • 1 Effect 1 Supply.
  • 2 Effect 1d3 Supply.
  • 3 Effect 2d3 Supply.
  • 4+ Effect 2d3 Supply and you find an item of your choice.

Survey

Choose a zone, then roll Sense.

  • On a 4/5, choose one:
    • You

      Travel

      Choose a zone. For the remainder of the day, you may perform any tasks as if your Base were in that zone.

      Rest (Limited to once per day)

      Spend 1 Supply. You heal all Exhausted harm, then reduce all harm. (All Level-1 harm is healed, all harm of a higher level is brought down one level). Then lose Momentum down to your Momentum Cap.

Downed PCs

A PC who has been downed must successfully rest before they can act during the day or night. Depending on where a player was downed, a Scavenge or Raid task may be required to return the downed PC to base.

Working as a Team

When two or more PCs perform a task together, they can choose to act as a Team by choosing a Leader.

Whenever the task calls for a roll, each member of the team attempts the roll. The result is the highest roll, but the Leader suffers Level-1 harm of the GM's choice for each PC who rolled 1-3 as their best result.
