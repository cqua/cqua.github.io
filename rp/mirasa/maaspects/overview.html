Major Aspects

[Information on Major Aspects will be added later]

Whenever an advancement allows you a new Aspect, you may choose one from the 12 options below, or if you already have at least one Major Aspect, a Minor Aspect. The paths described in this section represent the most common paths taken by people living in the world. Major Aspects are arranged in four broad groupings that evoke the four Starting Paths. Each one describes a route you can take to grow your power and ability. Although your Starting Path might suggest a Major Aspect, such as priests choosing aspects of will, you can freely choose any aspect you like.

ASPECTS OF WAR

  • Berserker unleash their fury in battle to become wild, killing machines.
  • Fighters train to become experts at a particular fighting style.
  • Hunters are expert rangers who know how to find, track, and destroy their prey.
  • Spellbinders imbue their weapons with magic to enhance their combat abilities.

ASPECTS OF TRICKERY

  • Assassins kill from hiding, using disguises and poison.
  • Bards are jacks of all trades, with a specialty in performance. A little swordplay, a little magic, and a little skullduggery.
  • Scouts are skilled in stealth and observation. They gather information for their allies.
  • Thieves develop useful skills to help them carry out their criminal designs.

PATHS OF POWER

  • Artificers create magical trinkets and servants.
  • Sorcerers harness incredible amounts of magical energy at great risk to themselves.
  • Wardscribes write the ancient words of power with special implements.
  • Wizards are academicians of magic, keeping their lore in great tomes called grimoires.

ASPECTS OF RESOLVE

  • Clerics use the symbols of faith to drive back and destroy the gods' enemies.
  • Elementalists are devotees to the primal who evoke all four elements.
  • Paladins use their faith to protect the innocent and smite darkness.
  • Strategists aid their allies with their wise words.
