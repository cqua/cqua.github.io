Elementalist

Carson Lowmiller

Rather than study magic like a wizard, or dedicate to a cause like a paladin, elementalist's magic comes from invoking raw mana. Elementalists often come from very rural backgrounds where they experienced the raw power of nature. The key to elementalist magic is to forget oneself, to lose oneself in the eternal flow of mana.

CORE ELEMENTALIST

Attributes Increase two by 1
Characteristics Health +2, Power +1
Magic Make two choices. For each choice, you discover a tradition or learn one spell.

Aeromancer Whenever you cast an Air spell, after resolving its effect, you can fly a number of yards equal to the rank of the spell.

Geomancer Whenever you cast an Earth spell, after resolving its effect, your defense increases equal to the rank of the spell. This effect lasts for 1 minute.

Hydromancer Whenever you cast a Water spell, after resolving its effect, you or a creature you can reach heals damage equal to the rank of the spell.

Pyromancer Whenever a creature takes damage from a Fire or Storm spell you cast, it takes additional damage equal to the rank of the spell.

EXPERT ELEMENTALIST

Characteristics Health +2
Magic You discover a tradition or learn one spell.

Elemental Attunement You can use an action to become attuned to air, earth, fire, or water. You remain attuned to the element until you use this talent again, you become unconscious, or you complete a rest. The benefits of this talent depend on which element you choose, as follows:

  • Air: You have a +2 bonus to speed. If you have discovered the Air tradition, the bonus increases to +8.
  • Earth: You have a +1 bonus to Defense. If you have discovered the Earth tradition, the bonus increases to +3.
  • Fire: Flames swirl around you, lighting the area within 5 yards of you. Whenever you take damage from a melee attack, the creature that attacked you must get a success on an Agility challenge roll or take 1d6 damage. If you discovered the Fire tradition, the damage increases to 2d6.
  • Water: You have a +5 bonus to Health. If you have discovered the Water tradition, the bonus increases to 15.
