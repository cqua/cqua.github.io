Minor Aspects

[Information on Minor Aspects will be added later]

After you've chosen at least one Major Aspect, whenever an advancement allows you an Aspect, you may select a Minor Aspect from the options presented in this section or choose a Major Aspect instead.

Minor Aspects are much more specialized than Major Aspects, and they represent an echelon that few mortals have reached. When you take a Minor Aspect, you are an expert in your field.
