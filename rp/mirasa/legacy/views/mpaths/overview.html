Master Paths

When your group reaches level 7, each member selects a master path from the 74 options presented in this section or chooses a second expert path. The paths presented here are organized into two broad groups to help you navigate the choices. Paths of magic let you master a tradition of magic or a method of casting, while paths of skill let you improve existing abilities or make available new ones.
