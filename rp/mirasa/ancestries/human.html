Human

Luca Bancone

Humans were the first mortal beings- all other mortals were once descended from humans. As such, they exist in just about every corner of the world. They are marked by their determination, resourcefulness, and sheer numbers.

Many Variations Humans comprise many different ethnic groups. Skin tones can range from almost black to albino white, or green, blue, orange, pink, or something else. Some humans have patterned skin, stripes, thick body hair, or no hair at all. Humans also have many different shapes and sizes. They range from 3 to 7 feet tall and weigh from 50 to 500 pounds or more. Most humans live about 70 years.

I Am Because We Are There is no distinct Human culture- neighboring city states have distinct cultures. While outsiders regularly confuse them, most humans take great offense to being associated with a different city state. The four largest stand in the cardinal directions. In the east stands tall the desert bastion Cairn. In the west the tinkerers and mages of Oeda (Sounds like Way-da) float through the air. The halls of Torig built by the ancient jotunn to the north are now inhabited by vagabonds and defectors, who have built their own society of misfits in the cold. The port city Waterkeen to the south is home to the church of Amaat.

CREATING A HUMAN

Starting Attributes Strength +1, Agility +1, Intellect +1, Will +1. Raise one attribute by 2 and two other attributes by 1.
Perception equals your Intellect
Defense equals 5 + your Agility
Health equals 8 + your Strength
Healing Rate equals one-quarter your Health, round down
Size 1/2 or 1, Speed 10, Power 0
Damage 0, Beasthood 0, Maximum Beasthood 5 + your Will
Language and Professions You speak the Common Tongue. Pick three professions or languages.

Heritage Choose one of the following options.

  • Western Increase one attribute by 1.
  • Northern Choose a creature type from among bird, clockwork, dragon, fish, insectoid, mammal, plant, or abberant. Once per round, when you make an attack against a creature of the chosen type, make the attack roll with 1 boon.
  • Southern You can breathe underwater, and swim at full speed.
  • Eastern When you make a ranged weapon attack against a creature suffering 2 or more afflictions, you make the attack roll with 1 boon.
