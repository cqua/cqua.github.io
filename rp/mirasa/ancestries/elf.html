Elf

Anna Lakisova

Believed to be descended from fae-touched humans, elves live in a grand kingdom of trees, glass, and magic in the forest.

Elves are humanlike, if significantly more thin and agile. They have a long series of social rules for etiquette that most elves follow thoughtlessly, but which often takes a lifetime for other ancestries to pick up. They stand 5 to 8 foot tall and weigh 50 to 300 pounds when fully grown. Elves usually live around 300 years, but are considered mature at 40 years. Elves' sexual dimorphism is very difficult to identify, and many non-elves assume they do not have it. Following, it's a tradition in many elven societies to choose your gender when you come of age.

Where human society often has little regard for nature, elven society seeks to expand with nature. This is both is caused by and leads to their closer connection with mana. Spellcasters are on every street corner, doing mundane such as aiding plant growth or working an elevator.

Common Names Doran, Estra, Mihaela, Nailo, Silvie, Toril, Vali, Viorica

Family Names Elves are more likely than other ancestries to have a family name which goes back millenia, and are difficult for non-elves to pronounce: Asgaiafor, Allwvidessus, Elynyoss, Harttyyl, Olthontor, Sardior, Suraphis, Tevtherioss, Ysglthrveyn

CREATING AN ELF

Starting Attributes Strength -1, Agility +1, Intellect +2, Will +1. Raise one attribute by 2 and two other attributes by 1.
Perception equals your Intellect
Defense equals 5 + your Agility
Health equals 8 + your Strength
Healing Rate equals one-quarter your Health, round down
Size 1, Speed 10, Power 0
Damage 0, Beasthood 0, Maximum Beasthood 5 + your Will
Language and Professions You speak the Common Tongue and Elven. Pick two professions or languages.

Innate Magic You discover one spellcasting tradition. See Spellcasting for more information

Keen-Minded You roll to resist the effects of Enchantment and Illusion spells with 1 boon.

Moonkissed You do not sleep, and are immune to the asleep affliction. When you would heal damage equal to your healing rate during a rest, your healing rate equals your Health divided by 3.
