Woodwose

Jeffrey Oyem

The ancestors of the woodwose made an ancient contract with the woods, to be its guardian.

Verdant Vitality The woodwose flesh mimics wood and bark- it tends to be tough and cold to the touch. Their bark can be any number of patterns, resembling fir or birch or maple. Woodwose can eat a wide variety of things, since rather than digesting, their bodies drain the nutrients. This means they can eat most plant and animal matter, as well as soil and wood (of course, eating any of these things live is a serious taboo). Woodwose will continue to grow for as long as they live. Most reach about 8 feet tall and 400 pounds by 100 years, at which point they die of old age. Many live long past that, continuing to grow larger.

Scattered Leaves Woodwose tend to be solitary creatures, wandering the wood, protecting it from destructive forces such as humans and fire. Some woodwose live their entire lives never speaking a word to another mortal. However, some woodwose choose to befriend or even integrate into human or elven society.

Common Names Woodwose outside of society have no use for names. When a woodwose is given or takes a name, it often describes their appearance, or a concept that is important to them. Some common ones are: Alba, Autumn, Estuary, Hallow, Leaf, Oak, Solstice, Sprig, Sycamore, Quercus, Willow

CREATING A WOODWOSE

Starting Attributes Strength +1, Agility +1, Intellect -1, Will +2. Raise one attribute by 2 and two other attributes by 1.
Perception equals your Intellect
Defense equals 5 + your Agility
Health equals 8 + your Strength
Healing Rate equals one-quarter your Health, round down
Size 1/2 or 1, Speed 10, Power 0
Damage 0, Beasthood 1, Maximum Beasthood 6 + your Will
Language and Professions You speak the Common Tongue and Sylvan. Pick two professions or languages.

Verdant Aura Plants flourish around you. Wherever you complete a rest, flora in the immediate area will flourish temporarily. Small flowers will break through stonework and metal will rust faster.
Additionally, whenever you forage for food in the wilderness, make the challenge roll with 1 boon.

Wildwood For 1 minute after you gain Beasthood, you make attack rolls and challenge rolls with 1 boon.

Iron Rot You can use an action to focus your mana into a metal object Size 1 or smaller which you are touching. The object rusts and shatters. Once you use this talent you cannot use it again until you complete a rest.

LEVEL 4 EXPERT WOODWOSE

Characteristics Health +5

You either learn one spell or gain Bestow Beasthood:
Bestow Beasthood When a target creature other than you that you can see within short range would make an attack roll or challenge roll, you can use a triggered action to grant that target 2d3 boons on the triggering roll. If the target accepts, it gains 1 Beasthood.
