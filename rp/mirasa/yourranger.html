Creating Your Ranger

SlothBeing

To create your Ranger, complete the following steps:

  1. Choose an Ancestry. Follow its guidelines.
  2. Choose a Starting Path. Follow its guidelines for enhancement to Level 1.
  3. You have 15sp. Pick out items from the Gear and Equipment pages up to 15sp worth.

Your Attributes are modifiers, which are added to attack and challenge rolls.

Strength represents your ability to perform strenuous tasks, such as moving a boulder, running for miles, taking a blow, or fighting off a disease.

Agility represents your ability to perform precise tasks, like dodging an attack, picking a lock, dancing, and hiding.

Intellect represents your overall knowledge and your ability to process and interpret new information. Many spells require Intellect as well.

Will represents your confidence and ability sway others with your speech. Many spells require Will as well.

Perception is your overall ability to see, hear, or otherwise sense your surroundings.

Defense represents your cumulative ability to block, dodge, or avoid damage from attacks.

Health represents your endurance, you ability to continue after a blow.

Healing Rate is how much your Damage decreases when you complete a rest, up to its max.

Size is how much space a character takes up. Size 1 represents the standard human, about 1 yard cube.

Speed is how many yards a character can move in a turn.

Power represents a character's spellcasting ability.

Damage represents your current physical state. When it is equal to your Health, you fall unconscious and begin making Fate Rolls.

Beasthood is how affected by the forest's magic you are.
