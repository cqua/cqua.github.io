Diruk

Algae which has grown tendons around stone, metal, and crystal to create a slow, lumbering, yet incredibly strong rock body. Can vary greatly in size, from 6 inches to 12 feet. Has no visible face, and while they can still speak, reading their emotions requires some skill. Reproduce asexually.

Origin: Kusyu

Specialties: Physique, Brawling, Demolitions
